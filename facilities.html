Everything You Need

Resort Facilities

From iconic swimming pools to bowling and event halls Grotto Vista Resort has everything for an unforgettable day out.

Swimming

Our Pools

8 pools in total 2 public pools for everyone, and 6 private pools for exclusive groups.

Flagship Attraction

The Galleon Ship Pool

Our crown jewel a breathtaking 1,600 sq.m. swimming pool featuring a full-size Galleon Ship as the dramatic centerpiece. Complete with water features, splash zones, and wide pool decks, it's the largest pool in San Jose del Monte and the heart of every visit to Grotto Vista.

1,600 SQM Area All Ages Welcome Public Pool
Public Pool
Main Galleon Ship Pool

The iconic 1,600 sqm pool with the Galleon Ship centerpiece. Suitable for adults and children with varying pool depths.

Public Pool
Secondary Public Pool

A separate public pool offering a quieter swimming experience, ideal for lap swimming and relaxed family time.

Private Pools × 6
Private Pool Villas

Six exclusive private pools available for rent perfect for intimate parties, team outings, and family reunions with complete privacy.

More to Enjoy

Complete Resort Experience

Beyond the pools, Grotto Vista Resort offers a full range of amenities and services for your comfort and entertainment.

Accommodation
Air-Conditioned Rooms

Comfortable, well-appointed air-conditioned rooms for overnight stays. Ideal for guests coming from far-flung areas or those hosting multi-day events.

Entertainment
Duckpin Bowling

A unique feature of Grotto Vista! Our duckpin bowling lanes offer fun indoor entertainment for all ages a perfect activity between pool sessions.

Dining
In-House Restaurant

Enjoy freshly prepared Filipino and international dishes at our in-house restaurant. Great for group meals, quick bites, and celebrating special occasions.

Events
Event Halls

Spacious event halls for birthdays, reunions, weddings, corporate functions, and team building activities. Our team can assist with setup and catering packages.

Nearby
7-Eleven Convenience Store

A 7-Eleven store is conveniently located near the resort, providing easy access to snacks, drinks, and daily essentials for your visit.

Rest & Shade

Cottages & Shelters

A variety of cottage options to suit any group size from small families to large organizational outings.

Umbrella Shed
Up to 4 people · ₱400

Open-air umbrella shed, ideal for small families or couples. Provides shade and a comfortable spot to keep your belongings while you swim.

Nipa Hut
5–6 people · ₱600–₱700

Traditional Filipino nipa hut cottage offering a charming and cultural resort experience. Perfect for small groups and families.

Train / Cubicle
Varies · ₱600–₱1,000

Fun and uniquely themed train or cubicle-style cottages. A hit with children! Great for groups who want something playful and memorable.

Pinoy Hut / Gazebo
15–20 people · ₱2,500–₱3,000

Large Pinoy Hut or Gazebo for big groups. Spacious enough for family reunions, team outings, and group celebrations with comfortable seating.

Roof Deck / Vigan House
15–25 people · ₱3,500

Our premium cottage option a covered Roof Deck or Vigan House-style venue offering panoramic views and spacious areas for large groups. Perfect for premium events and gatherings.
