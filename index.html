About Us

San Jose del Monte's Premier Resort

Grotto Vista Resort, established in 1995, is the first premiere in-land resort in San Jose del Monte City, Bulacan. For over three decades, we have been the go-to destination for families, groups, and travelers seeking world-class resort experiences in the heart of Bulacan.

Nestled beside the beloved Grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes and minutes from EB Town Center, our resort offers a unique blend of leisure, spirituality, and convenience that no other resort in the region can match.

Explore Facilities
Galleon Ship Pool

Our iconic 1,600 sq.m. main pool features a stunning Galleon Ship centerpiece the largest pool in the area, perfect for all ages.

Grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes

Located right beside our resort, the famous Grotto shrine makes Grotto Vista a unique spiritual and leisure destination.

Near EB Town Center

Conveniently situated near EB Town Center and a 7-Eleven, all your shopping and dining needs are just steps away.

Safe & Family-Friendly

Professionally managed and maintained for over 30 years, ensuring a safe, clean, and memorable experience for every guest.
