Getting Here

How to Get to Grotto Vista

Whether you're driving or commuting, here's how to reach us.

By Car
  • Take the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX) going north.
  • Exit at the Bocaue or Marilao interchange.
  • Follow signs toward San Jose del Monte City.
  • Head to the Grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes.
  • Grotto Vista Resort is located just near the Grotto.
By Bus / Jeepney
  • From Manila, ride a bus bound for San Jose del Monte (SJDM) from Cubao or SM Fairview.
  • Alight at the Grotto terminal or nearby stop.
  • Take a tricycle or short walk to the resort entrance.
By Tricycle (Local)
  • Tricycles are available throughout San Jose del Monte.
  • Tell the driver: "Sa Grotto Vista Resort, likod ng Grotto."
  • Most drivers in the area are familiar with the resort.
Helpful Notes

Landmarks & Tips

Key Landmarks Nearby

  • Grotto of Our Lady of Lourdes right in front of the resort
  • 7-Eleven located near the resort entrance
  • San Jose del Monte City proper a few minutes away
  • Accessible from the main SJDM road via short side street

Visitor Tips

  • Arrive early on weekends parking fills up fast.
  • Ample parking space available on-site.
  • Call ahead for group bookings and to confirm open hours.
  • Holiday rates apply on declared public holidays.
